Who is this for?

Food Tracker is designed for the average tech-savvy home cook. It's free to use, no app download required, minimally invasive, and no subscription fees (yet)!

How does Food Tracker work?

The Food Tracker system is comprised of a non-intrusive camera module that takes up minimal space in your cabinet or fridge, and our magical computer vision algorithm running in the cloud. Once you set up the hardware module and register your device, our computer vision algorithm runs in the background to identify objects in frame. Anytime you open or close the cabinet doors, it'll do a pixel diff between the last image and what it sees right now, and automatically update the database accordingly.

Okay, but how does it really work?

If you must know, we actually deploy a miniature elf into your cabinet and they send a carrier pigeon to the CIA headquarters, where we've hacked into the mainframe to constantly monitor your kitchen cabinets.

I'm ready, how do I start?

All you need is to register on our website, fill out your profile, register a device, put it in your cabinet, and boom! You can now see a list of what's in your kitchen cabinet anytime, anywhere!
